Construction
Our plywood is a multi-layer building material, consists of best selected veneer sheets glued together. We guarantee the absence of any impurities and additives. To glue the layers together we use an adhesive resin based on urea-formaldehyde. which provides the plywood with the maximum degree of moisture resistance and make it totally safe for any interior decoration. This adhesive mixture is the most environmentally friendly. It's confirmed by the appropriate quality certificate.

Key advantages of
Baltic birch veneer plywood

Sustainability
We care about nature and offer you a natural product made from renewable natural resources. Our plywood is perhaps the most environmentally friendly product on the market. 
Durability
Birch veneer has excellent physical characteristics. The multilayer structure provides a unique strength, which is almost 3 times higher than steel in terms of the mass / strength ratio. At the same time, plywood is as light as possible. The mixture used to glue the veneer also adds extra strength. Moisture resistance. Environmentally friendly urea-formaldehyde resin adhesive, based on natural ingredients, provides plywood with the highest degreeof moisture resistance.
Beauty
Architects and designers around the world appreciate the beauty of the Baltic birch. The natural characteristics and soft light texture of the wood are pleasing to the eye.
Versatility
The variety of products that can be made from our plywoodis impressive. The possibilities for furniture manufacturing and laser cutting are endless. Resistance. Museums around the world have antique pieces of plywood furniture that have stood for hundreds of years. Birch plywood does not lose its characteristics even at extreme temperatures from -40 to +50 degrees Celsius.
